# Workout Database

# Difficulty: Beginner

## Muscle Group: Chest
### Standard Push-Up
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Incline Bench/Wall Push-Up
- **Description**: Keep core tight and elbows at a 45-degree angle. Lower chest to the floor.

### Dumbbell Floor Press
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Glute Bridge Floor Press (No weights, squeeze glutes and chest)
- **Description**: Lie flat on the floor and press the dumbbells straight up, eliminating leg drive.

### Incline Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Elevated Surface (Bench/Chair/Bed)
- **Alternative**: Wall Push-Up
- **Description**: Place hands on an elevated surface to reduce the load on your upper body.

## Muscle Group: Back
### Doorframe Row
- **Equipment**: None (Doorframe)
- **Alternative**: Towel Door Anchor Row
- **Description**: Stand facing a doorframe, grip it with both hands, lean back, and pull your chest forward.

### Superman Hold
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Bird-Dog
- **Description**: Lie face down, lift arms, chest, and thighs off the floor simultaneously and hold.

### Prone Scapular Squeeze
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Banded Pull-Apart
- **Description**: Lie on your stomach, lift hands slightly off floor, and squeeze your shoulder blades tightly.

## Muscle Group: Legs
### Bodyweight Squat
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Chair Box Squat
- **Description**: Sit back into hips, keeping heels firmly on the ground and thighs parallel to the floor.

### Glute Bridge
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Single-Leg Glute Bridge
- **Description**: Lie flat with knees bent, drive through your heels to lift hips toward the ceiling.

### Reverse Lunge
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Assisted Lunge (Holding a wall/chair)
- **Description**: Step backward with one leg, lowering your hips until your back knee almost touches the floor.

## Muscle Group: Shoulders
### Pike Push-Up
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Decline Pike Push-Up (Feet elevated)
- **Description**: Assume a V-shape position with hips high, lower the top of your head toward the floor.

### Dumbbell Lateral Raise
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Water Bottle/Backpack Lateral Raise
- **Description**: Raise arms out to the sides until parallel with the floor, leading slightly with elbows.

### Wall Slides
- **Equipment**: None (Wall)
- **Alternative**: Floor Y-Raises
- **Description**: Stand flat against a wall, slide your back, elbows, and hands upwards while keeping contact.

## Muscle Group: Arms
### Chair Dips
- **Equipment**: Chair or Bench
- **Alternative**: Floor Tricep Extensions
- **Description**: Place hands on the edge of a chair behind you, lower hips, and press up using your triceps.

### Towel Bicep Curl
- **Equipment**: Towel + Heavy Object (or partner)
- **Alternative**: Backpack Bicep Curl
- **Description**: Loop a towel through a backpack handle, hold the ends, and curl upward against gravity.

## Muscle Group: Core
### Standard Plank
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Knee Plank
- **Description**: Hold a straight-line bridge position on your forearms and toes, keeping your core braced.

### Deadbug
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Bird-Dog
- **Description**: Lie on your back, slowly extend opposite arm and leg toward the floor without arching lower back.

# Difficulty: Intermediate

## Muscle Group: Chest
### Diamond Push-Up
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Close-Grip Floor Press
- **Description**: Place hands close together forming a diamond shape below your chest to emphasize triceps and inner chest.

### Dumbbell Bench Press
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells + Flat Bench
- **Alternative**: Deficit Push-Up (Hands elevated on blocks/books)
- **Description**: Lie flat on a bench, press dumbbells up from chest level to full elbow extension.

### Decline Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Elevated Surface (For feet)
- **Alternative**: Standard Push-Up
- **Description**: Place your feet on a chair or bench and perform push-ups to emphasize the upper chest.

## Muscle Group: Back
### Pull-Up
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar
- **Alternative**: Inverted Bodyweight Row (Under a sturdy table)
- **Description**: Hang from a bar with palms facing away, pull yourself up until your chin clears the bar.

### Dumbbell Single-Arm Row
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells + Bench
- **Alternative**: Loaded Backpack Row
- **Description**: Place one knee and hand on a bench, pull the dumbbell toward your hip keeping your elbow tucked.

### Inverted Row
- **Equipment**: Low Bar or Sturdy Table
- **Alternative**: Bed Sheet Door Row
- **Description**: Lie underneath a low bar/table, grip it, and pull your chest up while keeping your body rigid.

## Muscle Group: Legs
### Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Equipment**: Bench or Chair (For rear foot)
- **Alternative**: Forward Lunge
- **Description**: Place one foot behind you on an elevated surface and squat down deeply with your front leg.

### Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Single-Leg Bodyweight RDL
- **Description**: Hinge at the hips, keeping legs relatively straight, lowering weights down the front of your shins.

### Goblet Squat
- **Equipment**: Dumbbell or Kettlebell
- **Alternative**: Heavy Backpack Front Squat
- **Description**: Hold a single heavy weight close to your chest while performing deep squats.

## Muscle Group: Shoulders
### Dumbbell Overhead Press
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Half-Kneeling Single-Arm Backpack Press
- **Description**: Press dumbbells vertically from shoulder height to full lockout overhead while bracing core.

### Dumbbell Pike Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells (Used as handles)
- **Alternative**: Standard Pike Push-Up
- **Description**: Perform a pike push-up using dumbbells to allow for a deeper range of motion.

### Deficit Pike Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Elevated platforms for hands (Blocks/Books)
- **Alternative**: Regular Pike Push-Up
- **Description**: Elevate your hands slightly to allow your head to drop past hand level for greater depth.

## Muscle Group: Arms
### Chin-Up
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar
- **Alternative**: Underhand Inverted Row
- **Description**: Hang with palms facing toward you, pull up until your chin clears the bar to heavy load the biceps.

### Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Resistance Band Overhead Extension
- **Description**: Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands, lower it behind your neck, and extend upward.

## Muscle Group: Core
### Hanging Knee Raise
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar
- **Alternative**: Reverse Crunch
- **Description**: Hang from a bar and pull your knees up toward your chest using your lower abs.

### Russian Twist
- **Equipment**: Optional Weight (Dumbbell/Plate)
- **Alternative**: Bodyweight Russian Twist
- **Description**: Sit with knees bent, feet slightly off the floor, and twist your torso from side to side.

# Difficulty: Advanced

## Muscle Group: Chest
### Archer Push-Up
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: One-Arm Assisted Push-Up
- **Description**: Slide your body to one side during the push-up, keeping the opposite arm perfectly straight.

### Weighted Dip
- **Equipment**: Dip Bars + Weight Belt (or heavy backpack)
- **Alternative**: Feet-Elevated Decline Push-Up
- **Description**: Lower your body on dip bars with extra load attached, stopping when shoulders pass elbows.

### Ring Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Gymnastic Rings
- **Alternative**: Deficit Push-Up on Dumbbells
- **Description**: Perform push-ups on unstable gymnastic rings, forcing deep stabilization and inner chest activation.

## Muscle Group: Back
### Weighted Pull-Up
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar + Weight Belt
- **Alternative**: L-Sit Pull-Up
- **Description**: Perform a standard pull-up with additional resistance strapped or held between feet.

### Front Lever Raise
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar
- **Alternative**: Advanced Inverted Row (Feet elevated higher than hands)
- **Description**: From a hang, pull with straight arms to raise your entire body horizontal to the floor.

### Single-Arm Pull-Up Progression
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar + Towel
- **Alternative**: Archer Pull-Up
- **Description**: Grip the bar with one hand and a supporting towel with the other, putting 80% load on one side.

## Muscle Group: Legs
### Pistol Squat
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Alternative**: Assisted Pistol Squat (Holding a strap/pole)
- **Description**: Perform a deep squat entirely on a single leg while extending the non-working leg straight out.

### Nordic Hamstring Curl
- **Equipment**: Secure Anchor for ankles (or partner)
- **Alternative**: Heavy Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
- **Description**: Kneel down, anchor your ankles, and slowly lower your torso to the floor using only your hamstrings.

### Weighted Bulgarian Split Squat
- **Equipment**: Heavy Dumbbells + Bench
- **Alternative**: Bodyweight Bulgarian Split Squat (High Volume)
- **Description**: Perform the split squat with heavy dumbbells in each hand to maximize mechanical tension.

## Muscle Group: Shoulders
### Handstand Push-Up
- **Equipment**: Wall Support
- **Alternative**: Wall-Walks to Hold / Deep Deficit Pike Push-Ups
- **Description**: Kick up into a handstand against a wall, lower yourself until your head touches, and press up.

### Dumbbell Arnold Press
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Seated Dumbbell Press
- **Description**: Start with palms facing you at shoulder level, rotate hands outward as you press overhead.

### Ring Face Pull
- **Equipment**: Gymnastic Rings or Suspension Straps
- **Alternative**: Towel Door-Anchor Face Pull
- **Description**: Lean back on rings and pull your hands to your ears, flaring elbows out to crush the rear delts.

## Muscle Group: Arms
### Muscle-Up
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar or Gymnastic Rings
- **Alternative**: Explosive High Pull-Up + Dip
- **Description**: Pull yourself up explosively past the chest, transition your weight, and dip to full lockout.

### Dumbbell Hammer Curl to Press
- **Equipment**: Dumbbells
- **Alternative**: Backpack Hammer Curl to Press
- **Description**: Neutral grip bicep curl directly into an overhead shoulder press in one smooth sequence.

## Muscle Group: Core
### Hanging Leg Raise
- **Equipment**: Pull-Up Bar
- **Alternative**: Hanging Knee Raise
- **Description**: Keeping your legs completely locked straight, lift your feet all the way up to touch the bar.

### Dragon Flag
- **Equipment**: Sturdy Bench or Pole to grip
- **Alternative**: Lying Leg Raise (Slow Negatives)
- **Description**: Lock your shoulders down and lift your entire torso and legs into a straight line, lowering slowly.
